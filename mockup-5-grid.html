About Shakierah

Law school does not come with instructions. This is the roadmap.

Shakierah Smith is a first-generation law graduate, practicing attorney, and published researcher who has navigated every stage of the legal journey without a roadmap. She built The Pinkprint Lawyer to be the resource she wished she had — evidence-informed, honest, and grounded in the realities of what it takes to succeed.

Her work sits at the intersection of academic strategy and professional development, with a commitment to mentoring those who are building a legal career without the benefit of family connections or institutional insiders.

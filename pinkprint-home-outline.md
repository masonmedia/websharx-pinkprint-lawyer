Your Pinkprint for Law School, the Bar, and Beyond!
I am Shakierah Smith: a first-generation law graduate, practicing attorney, published researcher, and dedicated mentor for students navigating law school and the legal profession.
I created The Pinkprint Lawyer because law school does not come with a clear set of instructions, and far too many students are left to navigate complex systems without guidance. Here, you will find resources that are practical, evidence-informed, and designed to help you move through each stage of this journey with clarity, confidence, and intention.
This is not about perfection. It is about preparation.
